Column Name		Description
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
id			Employee id
satisfaction		Satisfaction level in percentile
last_evaluation		Employee’s last performance scare in percentile
number_project		Number of project being handled by the employee
average_monthly_hours	Average monthly hours worked
time_spend_company	Average hours spent in office
work_accident		Number of work accident encountered
left			“1” indicates that the employee has left the company
			and “0” indicates otherwise.
promotion_last_5years	Has the employee been promoted in the last 5-years. 
			“1” indicates yes and “0” otherwise.
sales			Indicate area of operations
salary			The level of salary
